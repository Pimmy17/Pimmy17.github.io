ABOUT ME


Since I don't have any form of social media for you to stalk,
I thought I would create this little section so you know what you are letting yourself in for!

My career to date has been varied, but it also shows that I am driven and I wish to be successful in whatever it is I do. The fact that I work quickly, but smartly and I want to be tested each and every day is why I am moving in to coding,
I am hoping that this will be my last career change until I'm much older and ready to set up my own B&B in Keswick.

So what else would you like to know about me?
I enjoy giving new things a go and exploring new places (see the slideshows below). I am a geek as I enjoy collecting coins (you can find me on Numista) and I can't get enough of the MCU films and TV shows, which you may find some inspiration in my solo projects with the colour schemes, or the blatantly obvious Avengers card game. On the more active side of my personality, I have enjoy playing football and golf, though hardly played either in the last 5 years. My wife and I enjoy hiking when the weather is decent, with countless walks in the Peak District and more recently we have started the Wainwrights last year (2021), currently on 21/214 completed as of April 2022.
